Areas of Focus

STREAMING ANALYTICS

Businesses are overloaded with constant streams of incoming data. What can all that data really tell you? What are the insights that can only be understood in the context of everything else that is happening? Complex Event Processing will allow companies to extract value from both their current enterprise applications, Internet of Things (IoT) products, and the next generation of mobile applications.

BIG & FAST DATA

Big Data represents all of the data flowing through your current systems, both structured and unstructured. Whether your organization uses a traditional relational DB or a mixture of persistence technologies, handling large volume/highly transactional applications means overcoming scalability and performance bottlenecks. We can help you overcome those challenges.

Tools & Technologies

APAMA

What to do with all that data? Apama is the industry leading streaming analytics/complex event processing platform that can help you attach real value to the constant stream of data your applications are processing. We can help you utilize Apama to correlate, analyze and wrap meaningful "context" around all of your streams of data.

Learn More
BIG MEMORY

App too slow? We'll help you achieve your scalability/performance goals. We'll help you achieve fast, scalable applications using the Terracotta BigMemory suite. We can guide you through the pitfalls of implementing a caching solution for highly volatile applications. A well designed cache helps eliminates the data-tier bottlenecks in your application and allows it to achieve max performance.

Learn More
PRESTO

Visualize all of your data in real-time. Presto allows you to create sophisticated dashboards and graphs that can provide you with a better understanding of your data and to extract the context that leads to certain situations.
